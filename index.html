Our Story

Los Gallegos is a small, family-run kitchen bringing the dishes of Spain to Miami — made the slow, careful way they were meant to be made.

It’s a true mom-and-pop place on Bird Road: the food comes out hot, regulars are treated like family, and every plate — from the croquetas to the paella — is cooked to order. Pull up a chair and stay a while.

The Table

A journey through Spain, course by course

From shared tapas to paellas built for the table, fresh seafood, fire-grilled meats, and desserts worth saving room for — here’s a taste of what we cook.

01 — Tapas & To Share

Small plates, big table

The Spanish way to eat: a table full of little dishes, passed around and shared over conversation and a glass of wine.

  • Croquetas de JamónCreamy ham croquettes with a crisp golden shell.House favorite
  • Gambas al AjilloSizzling shrimp in garlic and olive oil with a touch of chili.Tapa
  • Pulpo a la GallegaTender Galician octopus over potato with sweet paprika.Tapa
  • Tortilla EspañolaSlow-cooked potato, onion and egg, served by the wedge.Tapa
  • Tabla IbéricaA board of Spanish cured meats and cheeses.To share
02 — Arroces & Paellas

Saffron rice, cooked to order

Our paellas are simmered slow in the pan until the rice drinks up every bit of flavor. Built for two — or for the whole table.

  • Paella ValencianaSaffron rice with chicken, seafood and vegetables.For two
  • Paella Negra con MariscosBlack rice cooked in squid ink, heavy with fresh seafood.For two
  • Caldo GallegoThe hearty Galician soup of white beans, greens and pork.Soup

03 — From the Sea

Fresh catch, Spanish soul

Seafood is the heart of Galician cooking. We keep it simple — good olive oil, garlic, fire, and the freshest catch we can find.

  • Langostinos a la ParrillaGrilled jumbo prawns with garlic and olive oil.Del mar
  • Bacalao a la VizcaínaSalt cod braised in a rich Biscay pepper & tomato sauce.Del mar
  • Ceviche de CamarónShrimp cured in citrus with onion and pepper.Cold
  • Salpicón de MariscosA bright seafood salad in a light vinaigrette.Cold
04 — From the Grill

Fire, meat, and patience

Hearty plates for a bigger appetite — grilled and braised until tender, seasoned simply so the flavor leads.

  • Filet Mignon a los GallegosTender beef filet finished the house way.Carne
  • Chuletas de CorderoGrilled lamb chops, simply seasoned.Carne
  • OssobucoSlow-braised veal shank in its own deep, savory sauce.Braised
