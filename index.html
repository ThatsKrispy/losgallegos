Our Story

A small, family-run kitchen bringing the flavors of Spain to Bird Road — cooked the slow, careful way they were meant to be.

Good olive oil, fresh seafood, saffron rice, and time. Nothing here is rushed, every plate is made to order, and regulars are treated like family.

La Mesa

A feast, laid out on the table

From shared tapas to slow-cooked paellas, fresh seafood and fire-grilled meats — a taste of what comes out of our kitchen.

This is a taste, not the whole menu. Daily specials and prices change with the season and the market — call ahead and we'll tell you what's cooking today.

Hecho con Cariño

Made by hand, the old-country way

No shortcuts, no heat lamps — just honest Spanish cooking, plated fresh and served warm.

A Family Table

Pull up a chair
and stay a while

Los Gallegos is a true mom-and-pop place on Bird Road. The name carries the roots of Galicia and the wider Spanish table — a tradition built on good ingredients, patience, and a warm welcome.

Every plate is cooked to order, from the croquetas to the paella. The food comes out hot, there's always a glass of Spanish wine waiting, and there's no rush to leave. That's the whole idea.
