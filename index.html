A Family Table

Pull up a chair,
stay a while

Los Gallegos is a true mom-and-pop place on Bird Road. The name carries the roots of Galicia and the wider Spanish table — a tradition built on good ingredients, patience, and a warm welcome.

Every plate is cooked to order, from the croquetas to the paella. The food comes out hot, there's always a glass of Spanish wine waiting, and there's no rush to leave. That's the whole idea.
