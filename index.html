A Family Table

Pull up a chair, stay a while

Los Gallegos is a true mom-and-pop place on Bird Road in Miami. The name carries the roots of Galicia and the wider Spanish table — a tradition built on good olive oil, fresh seafood, saffron rice, and time.

Every plate is cooked to order, from the croquetas to the paella. The food comes out hot, there's always a glass of Spanish wine waiting, and regulars are treated like family.
