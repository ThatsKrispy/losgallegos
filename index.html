Nuestra Casa

A family table on Bird Road

Los Gallegos is a true family-run house in the heart of Miami. The name carries the roots of Galicia and the wider Spanish table — a tradition built on good olive oil, fresh seafood, saffron rice, and patience.

Every plate is cooked to order, from the croquetas to the paella. Nothing sits under a heat lamp; nothing is rushed. Come for a tapas lunch, stay for a long paella dinner.
