Our Table

Spanish cooking, the way it should be

Los Gallegos is a small, family-run kitchen serving the dishes of Spain to Miami — the croquetas, the paellas, the pulpo a la gallega — made the slow, careful way they were meant to be made.

It is a "mom and pop" kind of place, where the food comes out hot, the welcome is warm, and regulars are treated like family. Whether it is a quick lunch on Bird Road or a long dinner over a bottle of Spanish red, you will taste the difference that care makes.
