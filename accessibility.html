Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 2026

Los Gallegos Restaurant is committed to making our website accessible to as many people as possible, including those who use assistive technologies. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as a practical standard.

What we've done

  • Semantic HTML with clear headings, landmarks, and a "skip to content" link.
  • Keyboard-operable navigation, menu filters, and cookie controls, with visible focus outlines.
  • Descriptive alternative text on meaningful images and accessible names on icon buttons and links.
  • Color contrast chosen to meet WCAG AA for body text and interactive elements.
  • Respect for the "reduce motion" system setting — animations are minimized when requested.
  • Responsive, mobile-first layout with large tap targets and no reliance on horizontal scrolling.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing process, and automated overlays are not a substitute for accessible design. We build accessibility into the site itself and review it as content changes. If any part of this site presents a barrier, we want to fix it.

Contact us

If you have trouble accessing anything on this site, or need information in another format, please call (305) 661-3040 or visit us at 6549 Bird Rd, Miami, FL 33155 and we'll be glad to help.
